Objectively Correct Game Reviews

Articles

  • Platformers

    Action games in which the core objective is to move the player character between points in an environment.

  • FPS Games

    A first-person shooter (FPS) is a video game centered on gun fighting and other weapon-based combat seen from a first-person perspective.

  • RPGs

    A genre where players control characters within a game world, often engaging in a story-driven experience with character growth, exploration, and combat.

  • Open World Games

    These games offer players a large, interconnected, and accessible environment where they can freely explore and engage with various activities, without significant restrictions on their actions or the order in which they complete tasks.
